About Us

We Focus on building Strong, Intuitive and Dynamic Sites.

Free Website Design is a project dedicated to helping individuals start their journey in the digital space by providing free website creation services. We design websites for free, as long as clients provide the necessary images and information. Our services are tailored for informational sites with up to four pages.


We utilize free platforms and tools such as WordPress, GitHub, Cloudflare, and BitTorrent BTFS. Additionally, we offer blockchain smart contract deployment on any EVM-compatible blockchain. While the smart contract service isn’t free due to gas fees, we provide options through Thirdweb or Remix for those interested.
